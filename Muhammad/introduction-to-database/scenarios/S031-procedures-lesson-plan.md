# S031 — Procedures & functions lesson plan

## Tests

From the procedures & functions lecture, Fazah drafts a 45-minute undergraduate lesson plan and then
develops it iteratively — shorten, add a coding task, teacher prompts, an exit ticket, objectives —
before spinning off a student handout, a separate exit-ticket key, and a 5-slide outline, all
grounded in the one selected lecture.

## Auditor variation

You may change **exactly one** instruction in one turn (keep every other prompt verbatim). Pick one,
and record what you changed and how Fazah adapted — the count/constraint in the affected Expect line
moves with your change:

- Turn 1: change the length ("make it 30 min" / "make it 90 min") — the time breakdown must re-sum to
  the new length.
- Turn 1: change the audience ("for first-year students" / "for a graduate seminar") — depth and
  wording should shift, still grounded in Lec6.
- Turn 1: add a timing breakdown up front ("give me a minute-by-minute breakdown") — expect an
  explicit per-segment schedule.
- Turn 6: change the objective count ("add 5 measurable objectives") — expect exactly that many.
