### Metric definitions (the only six)

1. **Scenario pass rate** — (Pass + Pass with small issue) ÷ completed scenarios.
2. **Critical failure count** — number of scenarios with any Critical-fail trigger.
3. **Correct source-use rate** — Source use marked `Yes` ÷ source-relevant scenarios.
4. **Teacher-ready output rate** — Output marked usable without meaningful editing ÷ completed
   content/artifact scenarios.
5. **Conversation success rate** — Multi-turn scenarios handled correctly ÷ all multi-turn scenarios.
6. **Clarification success rate** — Ambiguous scenarios handled by correct clarification **or** a
   clearly-stated safe assumption ÷ all ambiguity scenarios.
