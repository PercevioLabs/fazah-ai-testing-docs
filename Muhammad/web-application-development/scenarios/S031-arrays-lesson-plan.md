## Auditor variation

Optional swaps an auditor may substitute for a single turn without breaking the grounding or the
answer-free rule (keep all other turns as written):

- Change the lesson length (e.g. 30 min or 60 min) instead of 45 — Fazah must re-fit the timing.
- Change the audience (e.g. first-year students) instead of undergrads — content stays grounded in the arrays deck.
- Ask for the timing breakdown earlier/later in the arc.
- Ask for a different count of objectives (e.g. 4 measurable objectives).

Every student-facing asset must remain answer-free regardless of the variation chosen.
